Serviços SPERO para profissionais

SPEROBuilding

Building cadastre and the categorized inventory (including photos) of georreferenced damages, obtained with in situ surveys using mobile devices - iPads. Spero uses scientific decision-aid models to prioritize interventions.


Target clients:

Private Organizations, Real Estate Management Companies, Real Estate Funds, Public Administration, Municipalities, and others.

SPERORoads

Road assets cadastre and the categorized inventory of georreferenced damages such as road pavement pathologies, obtained with in situ surveys, including photos, using mobile devices - tabletsr. Spero uses scientific decision-aid models to prioritize interventions.


Target clients:

Municipalities and others.

SPEROCity

Assets cadastre and the categorized inventory (including photos) of georreferenced damages, obtained with in situ surveys using mobile devices - iPads. Spero uses scientific decision-aid models to prioritize interventions.


Target clients:

Public Administration, Municipalities, and others.

SPEROWater

Network cadastre and the categorized inventory (including photos) of georreferenced damages, obtained with in situ surveys using mobile devices - iPads. Spero uses scientific decision-aid models to prioritize interventions.


Target clients:

Municipalities, Water Supply Companies and others.
